What is

Covid-19?

mild to severe respiratory illness that is transmitted chiefly by contact with infectious material or with objects or surfaces contaminated by the causative virus, and is characterized especially by fever, cough, and shortness of breath and may progress to pneumonia and respiratory failure.

Prevention

Stay Home

The best way to prevent illness is to avoid being exposed. Stay home as much as possible

Safe distance

Avoid crowded places and keep 6ft from others

Wash your hands

Wash your hands with soap and water for 20 seconds or use hand sanitizer that contains at least 60% alcohol

Cover your mouth

Cover your mouth and nose with a face mask or scarf when around others

Sick?

Follow local health authority guidance
Talk to a healthcare provider
Call ahead to confirm
